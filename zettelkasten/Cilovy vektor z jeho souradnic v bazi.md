# Cilovy vektor z jeho souradnic v bazi
Zadana baze
$$
\large
\beta = (x,y,z)
$$
a souradnice vektoru v bazi
$$
\large
(\mathbb{w})_\beta = (1,-1,0)
$$
Kolik je vysledny vektor $\vec{w}$?
$$
\large
\mathbb{w} = 1 \cdot (x)+(-1\cdot) y+0\cdot z\
\mathbb{w} = (x,-y,0)
$$
